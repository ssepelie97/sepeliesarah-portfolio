My hometown: Sint-Martens-Latem

Sint-Martens-Latem, nestled along the banks of the Leie River in Belgium, embodies a serene fusion of art, nature, and cultural richness. Renowned for its picturesque landscapes, this charming village has long been a source of inspiration for artists, particularly during the early 20th century when it became a haven for the Latem School. The essence of Sint-Martens-Latem lies in its idyllic surroundings, where lush greenery and meandering waterways provide a tranquil backdrop for creativity to flourish. The village's quaint streets are dotted with historic houses, some of which once hosted prominent artists. Today, Sint-Martens-Latem continues to attract art enthusiasts and nature lovers alike, offering a retreat-like ambiance that celebrates the harmonious coexistence of artistic expression and natural beauty.
